The Technical Blueprint

This instrument uses Additive Audio Synthesis to generate organic sound from pure mathematics rather than static recordings.

Harmonic Series

We layer three different sine waves at specific mathematical intervals to recreate the "hollow" timbre of natural bamboo resonance.

Portamento Glide

A 100ms frequency transition ensures notes slide into each other, simulating the physical movement of air within the pipe.

Impulse Response

A custom convolution engine simulates a virtual 2.5-second hall reverb, placing the digital flute in a real-world acoustic space.

Attack & Release

Exponential gain ramping prevents digital "clipping," mimicking the soft start and end of a human breath.

Note: Frequency mapping is tuned to the C-Natural scale for optimal clarity.

Our Vision

This project was created to explore how technology can produce not just sound — but calm.

Bansuri Pro Studio is an experiment in cyber-acoustics, combining Web Audio synthesis with the spiritual philosophy of Indian classical instruments.

The goal was not accuracy alone, but peace, breath, and flow.
